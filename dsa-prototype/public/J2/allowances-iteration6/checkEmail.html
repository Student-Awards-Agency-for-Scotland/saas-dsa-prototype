Check your email

The secure sign in link sent to your registered email will give you access to your DSA Account.

This one-time link may take a few minutes to arrive and will expire after 15 minutes.

We will never ask you for any personal information in our emails. We will only send you a link to access your DSA Account or for password resets.


What to do if you do not receive the email

  • check your email spam or junk folder
  • if the email is not in your spam or junk folder, request a new sign in link
  • if you still cannot activate your account, you can contact us
