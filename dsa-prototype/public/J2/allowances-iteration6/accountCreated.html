Apply for Disabled Students' Allowance

Account created

You have successfully created a DSA Account and can use this service to complete a DSA application on behalf of a student.

The next step is to:

  1. Search for a student record.
  2. Start or complete a student’s DSA online application.
  3. Recommend allowances for a student.
  4. Sign a declaration.

Once you have completed these steps, the student will be sent an email asking them to sign in to their SAAS Account to check the information provided, confirm their bank details, sign a declaration and submit the application. You can sign in to your DSA Account at any time to see the application status, and we will inform you and the student of the award decision.

Keeping your account secure


Never share your sign in details with anyone. You can sign out at any time and come back to complete an application.

As you will be accessing your advisor account throughout a student’s studies, please remember to keep your account information, including your email address, up-to-date

If another advisor will be taking over from your role, please contact us.
