Allowances

Consumable items

Total:   £200

General costs related to a student’s course and incurred as a result of their disability. For example, printing, ink and Braille paper.

Claimed yearly. Allowance limit per academic year: £1,725

Show more

Equipment, software and accessories

Total:   £3,189.60

Equipment, software and accessories required by the student to undertake the course. This can include computing equipment, ergonomics, assistive software and other technical aids.

Claimed for the duration of the course. Allowance limit per course: £5,160

Show more

Non medical personal help (NMPH)

Total:   £4,389.60

Personal helpers including note takers, mental health mentors, sign language interpreters and study skills support. It does not cover disability related expenditure needed outside of the student’s studies.

Claimed yearly. Allowance limit per academic year: £20,520
