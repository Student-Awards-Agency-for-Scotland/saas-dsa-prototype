What happens next

We will let your Disability Advisor at Glasgow Cale know that you have withdrawn your application.

You can still apply for DSA for this academic year. However, your Disability Advisor will need to start the application process again. If you change your mind about your application or you would like to discuss an application for the next academic year, please contact your Disability Advisor who can talk to you about the next steps.

To find out what will happen to your application and the personal data we hold, please read our Privacy Policy.
