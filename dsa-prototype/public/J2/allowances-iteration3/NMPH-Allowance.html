John’s Non-Medical Personal Help (NMPH)

Personal helpers including note takers, mental health mentors, sign language interpreters and study skills support. It does not cover disability related expenditure needed outside of the student’s studies.

Claimed yearly. Allowance limit per acadmic year: £20,520
