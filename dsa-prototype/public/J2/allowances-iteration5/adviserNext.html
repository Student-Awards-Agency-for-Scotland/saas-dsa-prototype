What happens next

We will let {John Gray} know that their Disability Advisor has completed an application on their behalf.

The student will need to:

  • check that the details on the DSA application are correct
  • sign the student declaration
  • confirm the bank account details of where they would like their award to be paid
  • submit the application

If they believe that any of the information is incorrect or they are unable to sign the declaration and submit the application for any reason, we will let you know via email.

Once we have received and processed the application, we will inform you and the Learning Support Team of the award decision via email. You can also sign in to your DSA Account at any time to see the progress of this application.

If you need to make changes

You cannot make any changes to the application until the student has signed the declaration and submitted the application, or rejected the declaration.
