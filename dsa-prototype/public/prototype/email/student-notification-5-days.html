Disabled Students' Allowance (DSA) Application Request - Reminder

We recently emailed about your Disabled Students' Allowance (DSA) application. Your Disability Advisor has completed an application for you, but we still need you to check the information and submit it. Please do this as soon as you can but don't worry; we still have time to process your application. If this is not done within 30 days from when we first emailed you, your Disability Advisor will need to start the application process again.

What happens next

Please review the information that your Disability Advisor has given us to make sure it is correct. This should only take a few minutes. If anything needs to be changed, please get in touch with your Disability Advisor who can do this.

Once you're happy with the information on the application, you will need to click 'I agree' on the student declaration page and confirm the bank details of where you want the fund to be paid. You can also enter a different bank account to the one we pay your main SAAS funding into, giving you the option to keep the money separate.
