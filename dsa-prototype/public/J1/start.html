Apply for Disabled Students' Allowance (DSA) service

The Disabled Studentsâ€™ Allowance (DSA) Account is a secure and easy way to access the online DSA application service. It lets you save an application as you go, so you can come back to the service when needed. It also helps us to keep the information you give us secure.

To use this service you will need a DSA Account. This will enable you to:

  • search for a student record
  • apply for DSA for a student
  • save and continue an application
  • add or verify a DSA recommendation
  • add quotes for the applicable allowance(s)
  • invite a student to create a DSA Account and sign a declaration
  • receive award notifications

To create an account, you will need:

  • an active email account
  • an email address associated with your higher education institution

It will take around 5 minutes to create a DSA Account.
