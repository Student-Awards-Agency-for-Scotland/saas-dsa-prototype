Apply for Disabled Students' Allowance (DSA) service

The Apply for Disabled Students' Allowance (DSA) service is a secure and easy way to apply for DSA on behalf of a student.

To use this service you need to create a DSA Account. This is a personal account linked to your college or university. Once you have created an account you can sign in at any time to submit a DSA application.

You do not need to upload any documents, including the Needs Assessment Report.

This service lets you:

  • search for a student record
  • save and continue an application
  • confirm a student's disability
  • add quotes for the applicable allowance(s)
  • provide details of the Needs Assessment Report fee
  • invite a student to create a DSA Account and submit a declaration
  • set contact preferences for your wider team
  • receive award notifications

To create an account, you will need:

  • an active email account
  • an email address associated with your higher education institution

It will take around 5 minutes to create a DSA Account.
