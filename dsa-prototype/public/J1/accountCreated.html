You have successfully created a DSA online account and can use the
Apply for Disabled Students' Allowances service.

Next steps:

  1. Search for a student record.
  2. Start a student's DSA online application.
  3. Recommend allowances for a student.
  4. Submit your declaration.

Once you have completed these steps, the student will receive an alert asking them to create an account or sign in to complete their declaration. We will then inform you and the student of the outcome of the application including details of the award.


Keeping your account secure

Never share your login details with anyone. You can log out at any time and come back to complete an application.

As you will be accessing your advisor account throughout a students' studies, please remember to keep your account information, including your email address, up-to-date.

If another advisor will be taking over from your role, please contact us.
